overview: "This project covered the cement-concrete lining of a 3.2 km nahar network serving agricultural land in Banda district under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) scheme. The existing earthen field channels were losing approximately 45% of water to seepage before reaching the fields. Wave Earth Infratech executed the complete lining works including bed and side wall concreting, inlet-outlet structures, and field drain connections - substantially improving irrigation efficiency and reducing water waste across the command area."
