# Page 05 - Equipment (`pages/equipment.js`)

## Purpose

The Equipment page is critical for tender submissions and contractor evaluations. Government tender specifications often include a mandatory list of owned machinery. Procurement officers check this page to verify:

- What machinery is owned (vs rented)
- Capacity and fleet size
- Age and condition of equipment
- Whether the company can self-mobilise without dependency on third parties

This page directly influences tender qualification scoring.
