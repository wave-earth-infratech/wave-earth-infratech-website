### Service 1 — Canal Construction & Repair

**Photo:** Active canal construction site. JCB or excavator working at canal edge.

**Description:**
> We execute new canal construction and canal repair works for state irrigation departments, including clearing, desilting, bank reinforcement, and structural repair of deteriorated canal sections. Our work is aligned with PMKSY and state Jal Shakti scheme requirements.

**Work Scope:**
- New canal excavation (earthen and pucca)
- Canal bank earthwork and compaction
- Desilting and deepening of existing canals
- Stone pitching and slope protection
- Head regulator and cross structures
- Canal rejuvenation under government schemes

**Work Process:**
1. Site survey and alignment verification
2. Mobilisation of excavators and earthmoving equipment
3. Excavation to specified dimensions
4. Bank formation and compaction
5. Stone pitching / RCC lining as specified
6. Final profile check and handover

**Machinery Used:**
- Excavator (1.5m³ bucket)
- JCB 3DX
- Tipper trucks
- Compaction rollers
- Water tankers for compaction

**Typical Client:** State Irrigation Department, Jal Shakti, PMKSY executing agencies

---

### Service 2 — Nahar Lining Works

**Photo:** Workers laying concrete canal lining. Close-up of lining work in progress.

**Description:**
> Nahar lining prevents water seepage losses and extends canal lifespan by 3–5 times. We execute cement concrete and HDPE lining works for minor and major irrigation channels as per BIS/IS standards and departmental specifications.

**Work Scope:**
- Cement concrete canal lining (M20/M25)
- Pre-cast concrete lining panels
- HDPE/Geo-membrane lining
- Side slope and bed preparation
- Expansion joints and sealing
- Lining inspection and quality testing

**Work Process:**
1. Canal bed and side slope profiling
2. Sub-grade preparation and compaction
3. Formwork placement
4. Concrete pouring and vibration
5. Curing (minimum 21 days)
6. Joint sealing and final inspection

**Machinery Used:**
- Concrete mixer (500L / 1000L)
- Slipform paver (if applicable)
- Plate compactors
- Concrete vibrators
- Transit mixer (for large volumes)

**Typical Client:** Minor Irrigation Department, Canal Officer, Jal Nigam
