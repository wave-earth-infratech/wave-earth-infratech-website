# Page 08 — Certifications (`pages/certifications.js`)

## Purpose

The Certifications page is a trust-builder used heavily during tender pre-qualification. Government tender committees and NGO procurement teams verify registrations before shortlisting contractors. Having all documents visible in one place:

- Eliminates back-and-forth document requests
- Speeds up tender pre-qualification
- Signals professionalism and transparency
- Reduces doubt about company legitimacy
